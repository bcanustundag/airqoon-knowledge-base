Purpose & context
Baris is co-founder of Airqoon, an air quality technology company focused on environmental monitoring solutions. The company develops scalable air monitoring systems with sensors that measure PM1, PM2.5, PM10, temperature, humidity, and pressure. Airqoon's mission centers on making air quality data accessible to communities, enabling people to reduce exposure, protect loved ones, and make informed demands from authorities. Baris views air quality as a fundamental right rather than a luxury and emphasizes community ownership of environmental issues over purely commercial approaches.
The company serves municipalities (with successful deployments in Istanbul and Bursa) and industrial facilities, offering rapid deployment capabilities with units operational in under five minutes. Airqoon operates within Turkey's regulatory framework, particularly leveraging R&D incentives under Turkish Law 5746 for employee benefits and operational efficiency.
