# Unit M - Maintenance Manual

<!-- unsupported block type: table_of_contents -->

# Introduction

This guide provides comprehensive step-by-step instructions for diagnosing and resolving connectivity issues with your Airqoon Sensor Unit M. The procedures outlined in this manual are designed to restore network communication and ensure uninterrupted environmental data transmission to the Airqoon cloud platform (Lens & Legacy Dashboard). These troubleshooting steps can be completed safely without affecting sensor calibration or measurement accuracy.

**Before You Begin:**

- Ensure the device battery is fully charged before commencing any maintenance procedures
- Allow approximately 15-20 minutes for the complete troubleshooting process
- Follow all safety precautions to protect the device's sensitive components
**Safety Notice:** Always power off the device completely before beginning any maintenance procedures. This protects both the equipment and ensures technician safety.

# Required Materials

- **3mm Allen key** (for removing and securing the enclosure screws)
- **Clean and dust-free workspace**
- **Anti-static wrist strap** *(recommended for protection against static electricity)*
- **Lint-free cloth** *(for cleaning internal components if necessary)*
# Pre-Maintenance Safety Checks

1. **Power Down:** Ensure the ON/OFF button on the device is set to the **OFF** position.
1. **Battery Verification:** Confirm that the device battery is fully charged to prevent power interruption during the troubleshooting process.
1. **Environmental Conditions:** Perform maintenance in a clean, dry environment away from direct sunlight and electromagnetic interference sources.

### 2.1 Reset Button Control

**Purpose:** This procedure reinitializes the WiFi module and establishes a fresh connection attempt to the network.

**Procedure:**

1. Set the ON/OFF button to the **ON** position.
1. Locate the reset button on the WiFi module as indicated in the device schematic.
1. Press the reset button firmly but briefly. A momentary press is sufficient; do not hold the button down.
1. **Status Monitoring:** Observe the LED indicator on the module for the following sequence:
  - **Flashing Blue LED:** Indicates the device is attempting to establish network connection
  - **Solid Green LED:** Confirms successful network connection establishment
  - **Brief Yellow Flash followed by LED Off:** Indicates the device has entered sleep mode after successful data transmission to the cloud platform
1. Set the ON/OFF button to the **OFF** position.
**Verification:** After the LED sequence completes, verify data transmission by checking your Airqoon dashboard for recent data updates.

**Purpose:** This step ensures proper SIM card seating and connectivity for cellular communication.

1. **Important:** After adjusting the SIM card position, repeat the reset procedure outlined in Step 2.1.

1. Carefully position the top cover, ensuring no cables are pinched or displaced.
1. Insert and tighten the four M4 Allen bolts using the 3mm Allen key.
1. **Torque Specification:** Apply firm, consistent pressure without over-tightening to prevent thread damage.
### 4.2 System Activation

1. Set the ON/OFF button to the **ON** position.
1. Allow 5-10 minutes for the device to complete its initialization sequence and establish connectivity.
