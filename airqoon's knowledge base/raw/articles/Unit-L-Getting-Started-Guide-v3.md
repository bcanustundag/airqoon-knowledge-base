# Installation

This document has been prepared to help you easily complete the required steps during the installation of the Airqoon Sensor Unit L and effectively monitor the control screens.

## Box Contents


The Airqoon Sensor Unit L consists of two boxes pre-installed on the main body, sensors, an antenna, a pair of wall mounting adapters, a solar panel, and necessary mounting bolts. Depending on your sensor selection, the sensor box houses gas, ambient, and particulate sensors.

The **Core box** houses the battery, core circuit board, and interconnect circuit board. The **Sensor box** contains the sensors and sensor circuit board. The main body consists of two parts: front panel and rear panel.

**Warning****: Before starting assembly, ensure the sensor unit is turned off by setting the switch to the 0 position.**

**Important Installation Considerations:**

1. **Residential Distance**:
  - The sensor unit should be installed at least **5 meters** away from any residence to prevent residential air pollution from affecting sensor data.
1. **Fixed Pollution Sources**:
  - The installation area should be free from fixed air pollution sources, such as factory chimneys or high-traffic areas, unless specifically measuring these sources. We need to ensure these sources won't interfere with measurements if they're not the target of monitoring.
1. **Obstacles and Shadows**:
  - There should be no obstacles that could interfere with the sensor unit's measurements. For example, a nearby tree could prevent accurate pollution measurements by blocking proper air flow.
1. **Solar Panel Orientation**:
  - For maximum solar panel performance, position the unit so the panel receives direct sunlight for most of the day
  - Solar panels should face south. This orientation captures maximum sunlight throughout the day and during winter months when the sun is lower in the sky
  - Shading: Ensure the chosen location for solar panels is free from shadows cast by trees, buildings, or other obstacles, especially during peak sunlight hours (typically 9 AM to 3 PM)
The Airqoon Sensor Unit L should be mounted to a utility pole using the wall adapters on the rear plane and the clamps provided in the box. For security reasons, it is recommended to mount the unit at a height that prevents easy access. The recommended installation height is between 4-6 meters.
