# airqoon Weekly Meeting Structure

## Purpose

This structured meeting format helps our team stay aligned, address blockers quickly, and maintain focus on our environmental monitoring priorities. Each meeting follows the same format to ensure efficiency and clarity.

## Meeting Format

### 1. Quick Check-in (3 minutes)

- Each team member shares what's top of mind for the week
### 2. Progress Update (5 minutes)

- Team members highlight key accomplishments since last meeting
- Review progress against previous action items
- Log recurring blockers in our issues database
### 3. Main Priorities/Requirements (10 minutes)

- Leadership shares top 3 company priorities
- Discuss any deadline changes
- Identify potential roadblocks
### 4. Open Discussion & Blockers (10 minutes)

- Team addresses urgent items and makes quick decisions
- Discuss items from the parking lot that need immediate attention
- Collaborative problem solving for technical or operational challenges
### 5. Action Items & Close (3 minutes)

- Clearly assign action items with owners and deadlines
- Quick summary of decisions made
- Preview of current week's focus areas
