# Certificates & Standards & Technical Specifications

This document provides a list of certificates, standards, and technical specifications related to air quality monitoring in Europe, Turkey, and the United States. The list includes guidelines for low-cost sensor deployment, performance evaluation of air quality sensor systems, and measurement uncertainty guidelines. Additionally, there are standards for automated measuring systems, ambient air, and fugitive and diffuse emissions. The document also provides links to relevant directives and supplementary documents.
