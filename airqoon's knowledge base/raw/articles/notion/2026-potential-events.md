**The SCEWC vs Ecomondo conflict is real** — both run Nov 3–6. They're in different countries and serve different audiences. My call: SCEWC Barcelona this year (municipal buyer density is unmatched), Ecomondo in 2027 when we have the Italian/Southern EU story built out.

**Pollutec Lyon is biennial** — next edition after the Oct 2025 one is Oct 2026, then 2027 again won't be it. So this year is the window. If budget is tight, STEP by Pollutec (Paris, Dec 1–2) is the affordable Paris alternative — smaller but sharp Northern European audience.

**GITEX Africa** — attending as a visitor is genuinely cheap (likely under €600 all-in if you can route through Morocco reasonably). Worth doing for the Gulf/Africa optics and to scope whether Togo is a repeatable pattern or a one-off.

**CEM Asia** — the co-exhibit angle with Amir/BCN Smart Tech is the unlock here. If you split the stand cost, this becomes very affordable and you walk in with a local partner who already has relationships in the room.

**Immediate deadline to flag:** SCEWC speaker application closes **April 16** — that's in about a month. Worth applying now even with an early-stage pitch.
