# Unit M – SIM Installation and Replacement Guide

<!-- unsupported block type: table_of_contents -->

### Introduction

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for replacing the SIM card in your Airqoon sensor unit M to ensure continuous connectivity and uninterrupted environmental data transmission. The procedure is designed to be completed safely without affecting sensor calibration or measurement accuracy.

**Before You Begin:**

- Ensure you have a compatible 4FF (Nano SIM) card with an active data plan
- Allow approximately 10-15 minutes for the complete procedure
- Follow all safety precautions to protect the device's sensitive components
***Safety Notice:**** Always power off the device completely before beginning any maintenance procedures. This protects both the equipment and ensures technician safety.*

### **Required Materials**

- **M4 Allen key** (for removing and tightening the enclosure screws)
- **4FF (Nano SIM) card** (must be active with an available data plan)
- **Clean and dust-free workspace**
- **Plastic prying tool or tweezers** *(optional – for removing the SIM card from the slot)*
- **Protective gloves** *(optional – for protection against static electricity)*
### **SIM Card Replacement**

1. Switch the ON/OFF button on the device to the **OFF** position.
1. Disconnect the power adapter to ensure the device is not powered.
1. Unscrew the **four M4 Allen screws** located on the top cover.

### **Test Procedure**

1. Ensure that the antenna is properly connected to the device.
1. Set the ON/OFF button to the **ON** position.
1. Monitor the device's connection process.
---

### **Final Installation**

1. Turn the ON/OFF button back to the **OFF** position.
1. Close the enclosure cover and **securely tighten the four M4 Allen screws**.

## Conclusion

Upon successful completion of the SIM card replacement procedure, your Airqoon sensor unit is ready to resume continuous air quality monitoring operations. The device will automatically establish connectivity with the Airqoon Legacy platform.



**Post-Installation Verification:**

- Confirm that the device status indicators show active connectivity
- Verify data transmission to your Airqoon dashboard within 5-10 minutes
- Check that all monitored parameters (PM, gases, ambient) are being reported correctly
