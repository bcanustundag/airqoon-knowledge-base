# Scentroid

**Scentroid** is an environmental technology company that produces air quality monitoring solutions, including specialized payloads for Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS).

## Key Product: DR1000 Flying Laboratory
The DR1000 is an integrated drone-based monitoring system designed to fly on heavy-lift platforms like the DJI Matrice 600 or Matrice 300. 

- **Sampling Method:** Uses an active carbon fiber probe to draw air from outside the turbulent rotor wash zone.
- **Capabilities:** Measures multiple gases and particulate matter (PM2.5/PM10).
- **Data Handling:** Relays data to a ground station tablet and cloud hosting platform.
