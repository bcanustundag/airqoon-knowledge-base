# Kunak Technologies

> Kunak Technologies is a prominent competitor in the low-cost / indicative air quality monitoring space. They manufacture sensor systems designed to meet "type approval" and rigorous certification standards.

---

## Products
- **Kunak AIR Pro**: Flagship multi-parametric air quality monitoring system. Measures NO₂, O₃, PM₁, PM₂.₅, PM₁₀, CO, SO₂, H₂S, and weather parameters. Known for high accuracy (meeting EU indicative measurement standards) and robust design.
  - Datasheet: [[wiki/sources/kunak-air-datasheet|Kunak AIR Pro Datasheet]]
  - User manual: [[wiki/sources/kunak-air-pro-user-manual|User Manual]]

## Competitive Positioning
- **Certification Leader**: Kunak aggressively markets its knowledge of and compliance with international standards ([[wiki/concepts/en17660-standard|CEN/TS 17660]], MCERTS, EPA Wildland Fire Challenge, AQ-SPEC). See [[wiki/sources/kunak-standardization-intro|Standardization & Certification Overview]].
- **Data Transparency**: They publish detailed co-location field study results showing impressive R² values (>0.85 for most gases and PM) and expanded uncertainty (U(exp)) well within EU limits. See [[wiki/sources/kunak-air-pro-performance-tests|Performance Test Results]].
- **MCERTS Certified**: Product Conformity Certificate for PM2.5 and PM10 indicative monitoring. See [[wiki/sources/kunak-air-pro-mcert|MCERTS Certificate]].
- **Target Market**: Aimed at professionals, cities, and industries looking for high-end supplementary/indicative data networks.

## Relevance to [[wiki/entities/airqoon|Airqoon]]
- Like [[wiki/entities/oizom|Oizom]] and [[wiki/entities/bettair|Bettair]], Kunak is a direct competitor to Airqoon's [[wiki/entities/unit-l|Unit L]].
- Kunak excels in demonstrating regulatory alignment and data reliability. Airqoon can learn from their marketing approach by actively publishing co-location results and educating prospects on standards.
