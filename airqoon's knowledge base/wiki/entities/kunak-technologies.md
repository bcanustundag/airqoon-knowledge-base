# Kunak Technologies

## Overview
Kunak Technologies is a prominent competitor in the low-cost / indicative air quality monitoring space. They manufacture sensor systems designed to meet "type approval" and rigorous certification standards.

## Products
- **Kunak AIR Pro**: Their flagship multi-parametric air quality monitoring system. Measures NO₂, O₃, PM₁, PM₂.₅, PM₁₀, CO, SO₂, H₂S, and weather parameters. Known for high accuracy (meeting EU indicative measurement standards) and robust design.

## Competitive Positioning
- **Certification Leader**: Kunak aggressively markets its knowledge of and compliance with international standards (CEN/TS 17660, MCERTS, EPA Wildland Fire Challenge, AQ-SPEC).
- **Data Transparency**: They publish detailed co-location field study results showing impressive R² values (>0.85 for most gases and PM) and expanded uncertainty (U(exp)) well within EU limits.
- **Target Market**: Aimed at professionals, cities, and industries looking for high-end supplementary/indicative data networks.

## Case Studies and Market Penetration
- **Port Authorities**: Balearic Islands Port Authority uses Kunak AIR Pro for environmental management (ISO 14001:2015).
- **Industrial Fenceline**: Cemex deployed perimeter monitoring networks in Mexico to protect population health. Shafdan WWTP (Israel) uses Kunak for odour and H2S control.
- **Urban / Public Sector**: Albacete City Council deployed Kunak AIR Pro and Kunak Noise within its EDUSI strategy. EFE Trenes de Chile monitors air quality in the Valparaíso metro.
- **Mining**: Gecelca S.A. deployed Kunak alongside Grimm monitors at Las Palmeras coal mine in Colombia.
- **Research**: US EPA utilized Kunak systems for wildland fire smoke detection challenge.
