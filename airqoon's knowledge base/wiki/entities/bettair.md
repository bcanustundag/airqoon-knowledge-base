# Bettair

## Overview
Bettair is a key competitor in the indicative air quality monitoring market. They focus heavily on simplified maintenance, transparent pricing, and robust certifications to win tenders and projects.

## Key Product: Bettair MK2
- **Measurements**: Gases (NO₂, NO, O₃, CO, SO₂, H₂S, NH₃, VOC, CO₂, CH₄, HCl), Particles (PM₁₀, PM₂.₅, PM₁), Noise, Weather.
- **Hardware Architecture**: Utilizes easily interchangeable sensor cartridges that are pre-calibrated at the factory, minimizing on-site maintenance.
- **Connectivity**: 3G/4G (pre-configured SIM free for EU), Ethernet, LoRaWAN.

## Competitive Positioning
- **Aggressive Certification Marketing**: Heavily markets its Class 1 CEN/TS 17660-1:2021 accreditation, US EPA performance targets, and MCERTS certifications.
- **Pricing Strategy**: 
  - "Zero Maintenance Cost": Factory calibration is baked into the price of replacement cartridges.
  - "Free Software": Data management and visualization platform subscriptions are included with the node and renewed automatically when a cartridge is replaced.
- **Data Processing**: Leverages machine learning technology for specific data processing to maintain stable correlations.

## Relevance to Airqoon
- Like [[wiki/entities/oizom|Oizom]] and [[wiki/entities/kunak-technologies|Kunak Technologies]], Bettair is a direct competitor.
- Their business model (bundling software and connectivity into hardware/cartridge replacement costs) presents a compelling "all-in-one" value proposition that Airqoon may need to strategize against, particularly in the EU market.
