# microAeth MA200

The **microAeth MA200** is a highly portable Black Carbon (BC) monitor manufactured by [[wiki/entities/aethlabs|AethLabs]]. It is designed for both mobile and stationary applications, including personal exposure monitoring.

## Technical Specifications
- **Measurement Method:** 5-wavelength optical analysis (880nm for BC, 375nm for UVPM).
- **Loading Compensation:** Features DualSpot® technology to correct for optical loading.
- **Filter Tape Cartridge:** MAFT-L17 (contains 17 sampling locations).
- **Flow Rate:** Adjustable between 50 and 170 ml/min.
- **Timebase:** 1, 5, 10, 30, 60, or 300 seconds.
- **Battery:** 3.6V, 3200mAh (11.52 Wh) internal rechargeable lithium-ion battery.
- **Charging Time:** ~3 hours (via DC barrel jack).

## Features
- Extremely small and lightweight form factor.
- Supports continuous unattended operation.
- Communicates via USB, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth Low Energy.
- Supports integration with external size-selective inlets (e.g., microCyclone).
