# DJI (Da-Jiang Innovations)

**DJI** is the dominant global manufacturer of commercial and enterprise unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). In the context of air quality monitoring, DJI's "Matrice" (M-series) platforms act as the primary heavy-lift carriers for environmental sensor payloads.

## Relevant Platforms for Environmental Monitoring
- **DJI Matrice 300 / 350 RTK:** The industry standard for enterprise payloads. Requires sensor inlets to be placed 0.40 - 0.80 meters above the body to escape the turbulent induction zone.
- **DJI Matrice 600 Pro:** A larger hexacopter platform. Due to massive downwash, sensor probes must be elevated up to 0.90 meters to reach the "clean zone" for accurate sampling.
- **Smaller platforms (Mavic Air, Phantom):** While possible to use, they offer limited payload capacity and require inlets elevated > 10 cm.
