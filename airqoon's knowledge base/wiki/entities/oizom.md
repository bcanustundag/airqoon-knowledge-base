## Competitive Assessment (Airqoon Perspective)

### Oizom Strengths
- Higher IP66 protection — better for harsh outdoor environments
- Wider operating temperature range (−20 °C)
- More connectivity options (LoRa, Ethernet, Satellite)
- ISO 17025 calibration certification
- More gas measurement capability (9 vs 8)
- UV radiation measurement

### Oizom Weaknesses
- Heavy and bulky (8 kg) — requires sturdy mounting
- Short battery backup (24h vs 14 days)
- Higher cost (>$8,000 vs $2,500–5,500)
- No public map — internal use only
- Complex maintenance and support
- Limited international presence reported
