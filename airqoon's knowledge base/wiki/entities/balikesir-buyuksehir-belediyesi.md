# Balıkesir Büyükşehir Belediyesi

**Balıkesir Büyükşehir Belediyesi** (Balıkesir Metropolitan Municipality) is a target municipal prospect for [[wiki/entities/airqoon|Airqoon]]. 

Airqoon's 2026 municipal presentation specifically targeted Balıkesir to pitch its mobile sensor network, emphasizing the limitations of relying solely on the Provincial Environment Directorate's fixed reference stations, and proposing an agile, citizen-facing environmental monitoring solution.
