# microAeth MA350

The **microAeth MA350** is a ruggedized Black Carbon (BC) monitor manufactured by [[wiki/entities/aethlabs|AethLabs]], built specifically for continuous outdoor environmental monitoring.

## Technical Specifications
- **Measurement Method:** 5-wavelength optical analysis (880nm for BC, 375nm for UVPM).
- **Loading Compensation:** Features DualSpot® technology to correct for optical loading.
- **Filter Tape Cartridge:** MAFT-L85 (contains 85 sampling locations).
- **Flow Rate:** Adjustable between 50 and 170 ml/min.
- **Timebase:** 1, 5, 10, 30, 60, or 300 seconds.
- **Battery:** 3.6V, 12800mAh (46.08 Wh) internal rechargeable lithium-ion battery.
- **Charging Time:** ~11.75 hours (via sealed DC barrel jack).

## Features
- **Weather Protection:** IP67 rated enclosure with dual o-ring seals on inlet and outlet custom fittings, making it robust against the elements.
- **Mounting:** Comes with wall mounting hardware and a sealing circular connector for DC power and serial communications.
- Extended filter tape capacity for unattended operation.
- Communicates via USB, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth Low Energy.

*For operational and maintenance details, see the [[wiki/concepts/aethlabs-ma-series-manual|AethLabs MA Series Sensor Manual]]*.
