# Barış Can Üstündağ

**Role**: Co-founder & CEO
**Company**: [[Airqoon]] (İNNOVATHİNK MÜHENDİSLİK SANAYİ VE TİCARET A.Ş.)

## Overview
Barış Can Üstündağ is the Co-founder and CEO of Airqoon. His responsibilities span the entirety of the business, from deep technical infrastructure engineering and IoT device management to high-level strategic sales, regulatory navigation, and thought leadership.

## Philosophy & Vision
- **Air Quality as a Right**: Views environmental intelligence as a fundamental public right rather than a commercial luxury. 
- **Community Empowerment**: Focuses on making data actionable so citizens can protect themselves and hold authorities accountable.
- **Product Strategy**: Positions Airqoon strictly in **ambient/immissions monitoring** rather than stack emissions or carbon accounting. He is exploring expanding beyond chemical/particulate sensing into visual monitoring (camera integrations).

## Technical & Engineering Scope
Barış manages a complex, full-stack environment:
- **Infrastructure**: DigitalOcean Kubernetes, Coolify, Proxmox, Traefik, Keycloak.
- **IoT & Data**: Migrating from legacy ThingsBoard 2.5.4 (due to strict MQTT header compliance issues) to TB 3.4.3. Manages PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and SigNoz observability.
- **DevOps**: Prefers preserving existing infra over recreation. Uses layered debugging. Uses n8n for workflow automation (Notion CRM + Ollama).

## Business & BD Scope
- **Sales Approach**: Consultative, relationship-first. Uses targeted cold outreach (Apollo.io) but favors technical discussions over hard selling. Prefers short, direct, warm emails.
- **Tender Strategy**: Deep understanding of Turkish procurement law (4734). Strategically uses the Domestic Manufacturer Certificate for a 15% price advantage. Builds documentation trails (e.g., chasing CEN/TS 17660-1 accreditation trails with VITO) to position Airqoon favorably in EU and domestic tenders.
- **Network**: Maintains strong relationships with academia (Boğaziçi, İTÜ, Univ. of Crete) for projects like MELTEM, and regulatory bodies (VDI/DIN, NEN).

## Notable Projects & Initiatives
- Driving the **Airqoon Brief** (brief.airqoon.com) to establish authority in Turkey.
- Navigating the **CEN/TS 17660-1** accreditation gap in Europe.
- Leading EU consortium proposals like **MELTEM**.
