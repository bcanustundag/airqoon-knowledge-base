# AethLabs

AethLabs is a manufacturer of scientific air quality monitoring instruments, specializing in Black Carbon (BC) measurement technologies. They produce the microAeth MA Series of portable and stationary black carbon monitors.

## Key Products
- [[wiki/entities/ma200|microAeth MA200]]
- [[wiki/entities/ma300|microAeth MA300]]
- [[wiki/entities/ma350|microAeth MA350]]

## Key Technologies
- **DualSpot® Loading Compensation:** Corrects for optical loading effects in real-time, providing reliable black carbon mass concentration data without frequent tape advancements.
