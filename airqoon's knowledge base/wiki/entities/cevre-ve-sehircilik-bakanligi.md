# Çevre ve Şehircilik Bakanlığı

**Çevre ve Şehircilik Bakanlığı** (Ministry of Environment, Urbanization and Climate Change) is the primary governmental body responsible for environmental regulation and the national reference air quality monitoring network in Turkey.

## Airqoon Collaboration
While municipalities often use Airqoon to supplement the Ministry's fixed reference stations, Airqoon has also directly supported the Ministry during crises. 

Notably, following the **2023 Kahramanmaraş Earthquake**, Airqoon deployed its mobile sensor systems to provide rapid, emergency air quality monitoring in the disaster zone, offering critical decision support when traditional fixed infrastructure was offline or insufficient.
