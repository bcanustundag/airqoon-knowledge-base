# Senseair

**Senseair** is a prominent manufacturer of True NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) carbon dioxide (CO₂) sensors. [[wiki/entities/airqoon|Airqoon]] leverages their technology for robust gas monitoring, specifically in harsh environments like underground mining where electrochemical and photoacoustic sensors may fail.

## Key Technologies
- **Senseair Sunrise / Sunlight:** New generation NDIR sensors utilizing LED IR sources instead of traditional incandescent lamps, drastically reducing power consumption (e.g., ~21 µA average for 1-minute sampling). 
- **True NDIR vs Photoacoustic:** In environments with severe pressure fluctuations (like mines), True NDIR is preferred over photoacoustic NDIR (e.g., Sensirion SCD4x), which can suffer from microphone interference.
- **Solid-State Design:** The Sunlight series lacks moving parts, providing exceptional resilience against vibration and shocks common in heavy industrial settings.
