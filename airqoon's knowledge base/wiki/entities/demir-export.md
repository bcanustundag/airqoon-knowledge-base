# Demir Export

**Demir Export** is a major Turkish mining corporation. In the context of [[wiki/entities/airqoon|Airqoon]], they are a target client for implementing underground environmental gas monitoring systems.

## Airqoon Implementation
The proposed solution for Demir Export involves adapting the [[wiki/entities/unit-l|Airqoon Unit L]] to withstand harsh underground mining conditions (dust, humidity, pressure changes, lack of solar power, and explosive gas risks). 

Key requirements include:
- Upgrading to an IP67 enclosure with sintered filters.
- Adopting low-power NDIR sensors (like [[wiki/entities/senseair|Senseair]] for CO₂ and MIPEX for CH₄) and long-life electrochemical sensors (like [[wiki/entities/alphasense|Alphasense]] dual sensors for CO/H₂S) to minimize maintenance and power consumption.
- Compliance with Turkish mine safety regulations for critical gases (O₂, CO₂, CO, CH₄, H₂S).
