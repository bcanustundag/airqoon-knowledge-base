## Measurement Capabilities

### Standard Parameters
- **PM (Standard):** PM1, PM2.5, PM10 total mass concentration
- **PM-Pro (Optional):** Particle size distribution (particle number)
- **Gases (up to 4 configurable):** NO₂, O₃, SO₂, CO
- **Meteorological:** Temperature, humidity, barometric pressure

### Optional Add-ons
- **Gas-Special:** VOC, H₂S measurements
- **CO₂** measurement
- **Wind:** Speed & direction via professional-grade anemometer
- **Noise:** Class-1 & Class-2 monitoring (IEC 61672-1:2013)

---

## Design Philosophy

- **Modular sensor cartridge system** — Sensors can be replaced on-site without removing the unit from its mount. No need to ship device for maintenance.
- **Solar autonomy** — Solar panel capacity can be scaled up or down based on deployment conditions. Smart power management enables 4+ weeks of autonomous operation.
- **Rapid mobility** — Lightweight and compact; can be easily redeployed to new locations as monitoring needs change.
- **Supplementary/indicative positioning** — Designed to complement (not replace) reference stations. Fills spatial gaps in existing monitoring infrastructure with hyper-local data density.
