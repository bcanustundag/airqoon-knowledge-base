# microAeth MA300

The **microAeth MA300** is a Black Carbon (BC) monitor manufactured by [[wiki/entities/aethlabs|AethLabs]], optimized for extended unattended continuous monitoring.

## Technical Specifications
- **Measurement Method:** 5-wavelength optical analysis (880nm for BC, 375nm for UVPM).
- **Loading Compensation:** Features DualSpot® technology to correct for optical loading.
- **Filter Tape Cartridge:** MAFT-L85 (contains 85 sampling locations).
- **Flow Rate:** Adjustable between 50 and 170 ml/min.
- **Timebase:** 1, 5, 10, 30, 60, or 300 seconds.
- **Battery:** 3.6V, 12800mAh (46.08 Wh) internal rechargeable lithium-ion battery.
- **Charging Time:** ~11.75 hours (via DC barrel jack).

## Features
- Extended filter tape capacity compared to the [[wiki/entities/ma200|MA200]], allowing for multiple weeks to a year of operation.
- Designed to integrate into stationary air quality monitoring networks or larger enclosures.
- Communicates via USB, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth Low Energy.

*For operational and maintenance details, see the [[wiki/concepts/aethlabs-ma-series-manual|AethLabs MA Series Sensor Manual]]*.
