# Cesva

## Overview
Cesva is a Spanish company (founded in 1969 in Barcelona) that manufactures sound equipment and instruments for noise measurement. They produce the TA150 noise sensor, which features class 1 precision and a variety of connectivity and integration options.

## Related Hardware
- TA150 Spectrum analyser sensor for noise measurement
