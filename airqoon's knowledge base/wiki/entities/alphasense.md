# Alphasense

**Alphasense** is a leading global manufacturer of gas and particulate sensors, heavily utilized within the [[wiki/entities/airqoon|Airqoon]] hardware ecosystem, particularly in the [[wiki/entities/unit-l|Unit L]].

## Notable Components Used
- **LFO2-A1:** A lead-free amperometric oxygen sensor with a 5+ year lifespan, replacing traditional galvanic (lead-based, 2-year lifespan) sensors. Essential for low-maintenance mining deployments.
- **COH-A2 Dual Sensor:** A compact "COSH" sensor that measures both Carbon Monoxide (CO) and Hydrogen Sulfide (H₂S) in a single 20mm body, conserving ADC channels on the Airqoon motherboard while minimizing cross-sensitivity.
