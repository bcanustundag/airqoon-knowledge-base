# Unit M

> Airqoon's portable, compact air quality monitoring sensor designed for indoor public and commercial spaces.

---

## Technical Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Dimensions** | 12 cm × 12 cm × 6 cm |
| **Weight** | < 1 kg |
| **Enclosure** | IP34 |
| **Power** | 5 V DC Adapter |
| **Battery** | 3.7 V, 7.6 Ah Li-ion |
| **Connectivity** | WiFi, BLE, 4G, NB-IoT |

---

## Measurement Capabilities

Designed specifically for indoor air quality (IAQ), measuring:
- **Particulate Matter:** PM1, PM2.5, PM4, PM10
- **Gases:** CO2 (via NDIR sensor), tVOC (via MOx sensor)
- **Meteorological:** Temperature, Humidity, Barometric Pressure

---

## Maintenance & Troubleshooting

The Unit M features an easily accessible internal compartment. 

**Standard troubleshooting sequence for connectivity issues:**
1. Open the enclosure via four M4 Allen bolts (using a 3mm Allen key).
2. Use the internal reset button on the WiFi module. The LED will flash blue during connection attempts, turn solid green when connected, and briefly flash yellow before sleep.
3. Verify the SIM card is properly seated and the antenna cable is fully connected.
4. Data transmission can be verified on the Airqoon dashboard within 10-15 minutes of rebooting.
