# Source: PACE Projesi

**Raw file:** `raw/articles/notion/pace-projesi.md`

## Summary

EU and German government (GIZ/BMZ) funded grant program targeting earthquake-affected Turkish municipalities. Airqoon positioned as technology partner for "Green Recovery" component, offering air quality monitoring during post-earthquake reconstruction (dust from demolition/construction).

## Key Details
- **Funder:** EU + Germany (BMZ via GIZ)
- **Application deadline:** 16 January 2026
- **Focus:** Participatory, inclusive, and green improvement of local public services
- **Target provinces (11):** Adana, Adıyaman, Diyarbakır, Elazığ, Gaziantep, Hatay, Kahramanmaraş, Kilis, Malatya, Osmaniye, Şanlıurfa
- **Priority areas:** Adıyaman, Gaziantep (İslahiye/Nurdağı), Hatay, Kahramanmaraş, Malatya
- **Airqoon's proposition:** Technology partner; all equipment, platform, installation covered by grant; municipality provides coordination only
