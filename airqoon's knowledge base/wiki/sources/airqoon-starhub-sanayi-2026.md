# Airqoon StarHub Sanayi Pitch (14 May 2026)

**Source File:** `raw/articles/Airqoon_StarHub_Sanayi_14May2026-final.pptx`
**Event:** İstanbul StarHub — Kurum-Girişim İş Birlikleri
**Presenter:** Barış Can Üstündağ (Kurucu Ortak & CEO)
**Date:** 14 Mayıs 2026
**Format:** 9 slides (Google Slides → PPTX), audience: corporate / industrial decision-makers

## Summary

An industry-focused pitch deck delivered at the İstanbul StarHub corporate–startup collaboration event. Unlike the earlier municipality-focused presentation (02-2026), this deck targets **industrial clients** — specifically cement plants and petrochemical / wastewater facilities — and frames Airqoon's value around **complaint management, regulatory compliance (CSRD/ESRS E2), and green financing (EBRD/IFC PS3)**. The narrative arc follows a pain → solution → proof → value → credibility structure.

## Slide-by-Slide Breakdown

### Slide 1 — Title
- **Tagline:** "Yeni nesil çevresel ölçüm ve yönetim sistemi." (Next-gen environmental measurement and management system.)
- Presenter info: Barış Can Üstündağ, Kurucu Ortak & CEO.

### Slide 2 — Problem Statement
- **Frame:** "Yönetilmeyen risk, ölçülemeyen maliyet" (Unmanaged risk = unmeasurable cost)
- Four pain pillars:
  1. **Şikayet ve itibar** (Complaints & reputation) — Even if regulations are met, community complaints + surprise inspections + public pressure remain a threat. Facilities emitting dust, odour, or gas need to document what happens at the perimeter.
  2. **İmisyon kontrolü** (Immission control) — Cannot control what you don't measure; continuous monitoring must be part of operations.
  3. **İhracat ve ESG raporlaması** — CSRD/ESRS scope companies must disclose ESRS E2 (pollution).
  4. **Yeşil finansman** — EBRD/IFC credit, green bonds, and sustainability incentives require continuous monitoring data.

### Slide 3 — Solution: Five-Step Lifecycle
- **"Tesisinizin çevresinde gözünüz oluyoruz."** (We become your eyes around your facility.)
- Monitors: dust, gas, odour, noise, meteorology.
- Five-step cycle:
  1. **Ölçüm** — Field sensor units
  2. **Görselleştirme** — Single-pane dashboard
  3. **Analiz** — Trend & source attribution
  4. **Aksiyon** — Alarms & reports
  5. **Değerlendirme** — Monthly summary

### Slide 4 — Lens: Single Pane
- **"Airqoon Lens — Çevresel veri içgörüleri üretir."** (Produces environmental data insights.)
- Operations + environment team → map + threshold alarms; management → monthly auto-summary.
- Three highlighted features:
  - **Kaynak tahmini** (Source estimation) — Wind rose + sensor topology shows which direction an event came from and which node triggered.
  - **Eşik alarmı** (Threshold alarm) — SMS, e-mail, or integration notification when user-defined limits are breached.
  - **Otomatik rapor** — Monthly summary, audit file, ESG report-ready data output. CSV/API access.

### Slide 5 — Use Case: Cement Plant
- **Title:** "Çimento tesisinde: oluşan tozun nereden ve neden oluştuğunu bilmek."
- **Operasyonel Sorun:** Clinker stockyard, coal storage, truck traffic — dust exits the gate. ~2 community complaints/month → each complaint = inspection + reputational risk.
- **Çözüm:**
  - PM + meteorology sensor network around facility perimeter
  - Real-time wind–PM correlation: which source, which direction, which shift
  - Alarm to operations team within minutes; control room verifies in-field
  - When complaint is filed: concrete, timestamped, defensible records
- **Verified Results** (KPI box):
  - **80%** complaint reduction (from 2/month → 0.4/month)
  - **Days → Minutes** event-to-response time
  - Framed as: "Reaktiften proaktife — kapanan döngü" (Reactive to proactive — closing the loop)

### Slide 6 — Use Case: Petrochemical / WWTP
- **Title:** "Arıtma / petrokimya: koku, toz, gaz ve meteorolojinin birlikte izlenmesi."
- **Two-column layout:**
  - **Left — Fenceline monitoring:**
    - Continuous measurement around facility perimeter
    - Odour and VOC events: location, duration, intensity, wind data at the moment of occurrence — most common source of community complaints
    - Dust and particulate: on-site dusty operations, truck traffic, storage areas
    - Meteorological dispersion: wind–pollutant correlation, atmospheric stability, temperature inversion
  - **Right — Reporting infrastructure:**
    - **ESG / Sustainability reporting:** CSRD / ESRS E1 (climate) and E2 (pollution) — continuous, auditable emission records
    - **EBRD / IFC credit conditions:** Ready data stream for green finance compliance, accessible via API
    - **Audit and transparency:** Concrete, timestamped data for regulatory inspections and public relations

### Slide 7 — Value: Single Data, Multiple Compliance
- **"Çevresel ağ ile dış yükümlülüklerinizi besleyin."** (Feed your external obligations with your environmental network.)
- Single data source → three compliance destinations:
  1. **AB · Raporlama** — CSRD / ESRS E1 (climate) · E2 (pollution): mandatory sustainability disclosure
  2. **Finansman** — EBRD / IFC PS3: continuous monitoring as green credit condition
  3. **Marka** — Community trust and transparency via Public Map, complaint management, PR

### Slide 8 — References & Compliance (labeled "9/10")
- **Referanslarımız:** Client logos displayed (7 image placeholders — likely including Bursa, Denizli, ministry, possibly Çimsa and others).
- **Doğrulama & Uyum:**
  - **Veri kalitesi:** Calibration processes configured per CEN/TS 17660-1:2021 and EPA/600/R-20/280. Output: indicative monitoring class, near-reference quality data.
  - **EBRD Green Technology Selector:** Airqoon's field units are listed in the EBRD Green Technology Selector catalog — pre-approved technology eligible for EBRD financing via local financial institutions.

## Key Strategic Observations

### Narrative Shift from Municipality Deck
The earlier Belediye Sunumu (02-2026) positioned Airqoon as an *alternative to expensive reference stations* for municipalities. This StarHub deck repositions for **industrial clients** by:
- Leading with **risk/cost language** instead of "monitoring gaps"
- Framing value through **complaint reduction** and **response-time acceleration** (quantified KPIs)
- Emphasizing **regulatory compliance chain** (CSRD → EBRD → public trust)
- Using sector-specific case studies (cement, petrochemical) instead of city-wide deployments

### New Claims & Data Points
- **80% complaint reduction** at cement plant deployments (first quantified outcome in any Airqoon presentation)
- **2 → 0.4 complaints/month** reduction metric
- **Days → Minutes** event-to-response time improvement
- **EBRD Green Technology Selector listing** — first time this credential is highlighted prominently in a pitch
- **EPA/600/R-20/280** calibration reference alongside CEN/TS 17660 (first mention of US EPA reference in presentations)

### Compliance Trident (New Framing)
The deck introduces a "single data, triple compliance" value proposition:
1. **EU regulatory** — CSRD/ESRS E2 mandatory pollution disclosure
2. **Green financing** — EBRD/IFC PS3 continuous monitoring as loan covenant
3. **Brand & reputation** — Community transparency, complaint management

### Notable Omissions
- No pricing or packaging information
- No Unit L hardware specs (deliberate — the deck sells outcomes, not hardware)
- No mention of Unit M or indoor monitoring
- Slide "8/10" appears to have been removed from the final version (deck jumps from 7/10 to 9/10)
