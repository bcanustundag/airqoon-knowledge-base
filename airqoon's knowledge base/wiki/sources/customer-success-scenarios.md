## Summary

Internal sales document defining target customer personas and success scenarios. Maps 11 use cases to 5 customer segments, with detailed regulatory context for Turkish market.

## Use Cases Catalogued
1. Perimeter monitoring (industrial)
2. Public information & awareness
3. Urban monitoring networks (spatial expansion)
4. Short-time air monitoring
5. Heat island effect measurement
6. Detecting pollution sources
7. Identifying hotspots
8. Assessing green tech investment impact
9. Leakage detection
10. Measuring intervention impact
11. Validating air quality models

## Customer Personas

| # | Target | Key Scenario |
|---|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **Facility owner** | Regulatory compliance, Çevre İl Müdürlüğü inspections, cost-effective alternative to reference stations |
| 2 | **Government/regulator** (Bakanlık, İl Müdürlükleri) | Expanding monitoring network, enforcing continuous measurement on facilities |
| 3 | **Municipality** (Belediyeler) | Public health, smart city PR, urban network expansion |
| 4 | **OIZ management** | Perimeter monitoring, source/hotspot detection |
| 5 | **NGOs** | Environmental impact projects |
| ? | **Çevre Laboratuvarları** | Potential but uncertain |

## Key Insight
CEN TS 17660 and EPA standards are cited as the regulatory enabler — once adopted, indicative sensors become officially "usable" as alternatives to expensive reference stations.
