# Ülkü Alver Şahin Academic Partnerships & Projects

This document serves as an index and summary of the joint academic research projects, proposals, and sensor specifications developed in collaboration with **Prof. Dr. Ülkü Alver Şahin** (İstanbul Üniversitesi-Cerrahpaşa, Çevre Mühendisliği Bölümü). The raw documents are archived in `raw/articles/UlkuAlverSahin/`.

## 1. 2024 Istanbul University ADEP Project
**Title:** Hava Kirleticilerin ve Sera Gazlarının Düşük Maliyetli Sensörlerle İzlenmesi ve Atmosferik Kirletici Kaynakların Belirlenmesi İçin Yöntem Geliştirilmesi
- **Scope:** A 36-month ADEP (Araştırma Destek Projesi) utilizing low-cost sensors to monitor air pollutants and greenhouse gases.
- **Key Documents:** 
  - `ADEP_Basvuru Formu_UAS_2.docx` (Project Application)
  - `HAVA KİRLETİCİLERİ ÖLÇÜM SENSÖR MODÜLÜ TEKNİK ŞARTNAMESİ v2.docx`: Details Airqoon sensor technical specs required for the project (e.g., OPC-N3 PM sensor with $r^2=0.9$, SO2 and NO2 electrochemical sensors with LODs of 5 ppb and 2 ppb respectively).
  - Various quotes and proformas (`Airqoon_PI_v2_TR_...`).

## 2. 2209-B TÜBİTAK Project

## 3. 2025 İnegöl Industrial Zone Modeling
- **Title:** Insight into LCS-based Air Quality Monitoring Campaign and CFD Modeling in an Organized Industrial Area (Spatiotemporal Assessment of PM)
- **Scope:** Academic article draft focused on LCS (Low-Cost Sensor) networks and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) modeling in the İnegöl Organized Industrial Zone. Includes co-authors from Embry-Riddle, UNLV, and the Ministry of Environment.
- **Key Documents:**
  - `sensor_inegol L 3_UAS _FK_ZCA.docx`
  - `BCUstundag CAyvaz, UASahin, Spatiotemporal Assessment...mp4` (Presentation Video)

## 4. 2025 Methane (CH4) & GHG Monitoring
- **Scope:** Specifications and quotations for adding advanced greenhouse gas (GHG) sensors to the Airqoon systems, specifically focusing on Methane ($CH_4$).
- **Key Documents:**
  - `Gasboard-2502 TDLAS CH4 Sensor Specification.pdf`: Technical details for Tunable Diode Laser Absorption Spectroscopy (TDLAS) sensors for high-accuracy methane detection.
  - `AM5303 Methane Leakage Detector Specification.pdf`
  - GHG monitoring quotes (`2025-Airqoon_Quote_Short_v3_1_TR_Ulku_Alver_Sahin_GHG.pdf`).
