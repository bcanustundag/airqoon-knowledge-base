# Kunak Cartridge VOCs Response Factor

## Summary
Information regarding the VOCs Response Factor for Kunak cartridges. It uses a Photoionization Detector (PID) with a 10.6 eV lamp, and warns about placing it near O3, NO2 or SO2 sensors due to ozone generation.
