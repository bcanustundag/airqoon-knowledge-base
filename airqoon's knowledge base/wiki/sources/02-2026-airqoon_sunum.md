# Airqoon Belediye Sunumu (02-2026)

**Source File:** `raw/articles/02-2026-Airqoon_Sunum.pdf`

## Summary
A sales and marketing presentation deck (dated February 2026) targeted at metropolitan municipalities (e.g., Balıkesir Büyükşehir Belediyesi). The deck outlines Airqoon's end-to-end environmental monitoring value proposition, positioning mobile sensor networks as a flexible, data-driven alternative to expensive, static reference stations managed by provincial directorates.

## Key Offerings & Value Proposition
- **Core Problem Solved:** Traditional reference stations are costly and their data is often controlled by the Provincial Environment Directorate, leaving municipalities unable to develop their own hyper-local environmental policies. Airqoon provides an independent, flexible monitoring layer.
- **4-Pillar Solution:**
  1. **Mobile Sensor System:** Measures PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, O₃, SO₂, CO, meteorology, and noise. Certified to CEN/TS 17660-1 & -2.
  2. **Citizen Interface:** Public maps (e.g., map.airqoon.com/bursa, /istanbul, /denizli) to build trust and transparency.
  3. **City Screens (Widgets):** Easy integration into existing municipal websites.
  4. **AI-Supported Reports:** Airqoon Lens generates 12-section automated monthly PDF reports featuring pollution source detection, trend analysis, and actionable policy recommendations.

## Reference Case Studies
- **Bursa Büyükşehir Belediyesi:** Comprehensive air quality network covering both metropolitan and district levels. Notably includes **forest fire tracking** and noise measurement.
- **Denizli Büyükşehir Belediyesi:** Focused on traffic-induced air and noise pollution monitoring.
- **Çevre ve Şehircilik Bakanlığı (Ministry of Environment):** Rapid emergency deployment following the 2023 Kahramanmaraş earthquake, providing critical decision support when traditional infrastructure was compromised.

## Certifications Emphasized
- CEN/TS 17660-1 (Gas)
- CEN/TS 17660-2 (Particulate Matter)
- ISO 27001 (Information Security)
- "Near-Reference" data quality
