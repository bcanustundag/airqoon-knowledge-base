# Airqoon Top 5 Use Cases (Sales Reference)

This document summarizes the five strongest commercial use cases for Airqoon's environmental monitoring hardware and software, designed as a practical reference for sales conversations.

## Core Products Addressed
- **Unit L**: Outdoor air quality sensor unit (PM, gases, meteorology, solar-powered, 4G/NB-IoT).
- **Unit M**: Indoor air quality sensor unit (PM, CO2, VOC, T/RH, WiFi/4G).
- **Unit N**: Network-based environmental noise sensor (IEC 61672 compliant, does not record audio).
- **Airqoon Lens**: Cloud SaaS platform (dashboards, AI-powered reporting, source attribution).
- **Public Map**: Citizen-facing web interface for public transparency.

## Top 5 Use Cases

### 1. Industrial Fenceline & Environmental Impact Monitoring
- **Target Sectors**: Cement, Mining & Quarries, Oil & Gas, Iron & Steel, Power Generation, Chemical Plants.
- **Problem**: Fugitive dust and diffuse gas emissions aren't captured by stack monitoring, leading to community complaints and regulatory penalties.
- **Solution**: A network of outdoor sensor units along the fenceline with anemometers. Lens correlates pollution spikes with wind direction to identify the source operational activity.
- **Outcomes**: Move from reactive complaint handling to proactive pollution prevention; up to 80% reduction in community complaints; response time reduced to minutes.

### 2. Municipal Air Quality & Noise Monitoring Network
- **Target Sectors**: Metropolitan Municipalities, District Municipalities, Environmental Agencies, Smart City Programs.
- **Problem**: Lack of neighborhood-level pollution data to address complaints or demonstrate the effectiveness of environmental policies.
- **Solution**: Dense city-wide monitoring networks (5-30+ units). Public Map for citizen transparency and Lens for internal municipal hotspot identification and policy tracking.
- **Outcomes**: Data-driven prioritization of interventions; transparent public communication; proven deployments in Bursa, Kadıköy, Mudanya, İnegöl, Avcılar, Denizli.

### 3. Indoor Air Quality for Public & Commercial Spaces
- **Target Sectors**: Shopping Malls, Schools & Universities, Entertainment Centers, Hospitals, Corporate Offices, Hotels.
- **Problem**: Poor indoor air quality (CO2, VOCs, PM) causing liability, health concerns, and poor ESG reporting without continuous visibility into HVAC performance.
- **Solution**: Compact Unit M sensors across high-traffic zones, integrated with BMS or standalone, providing real-time dashboards and automated alerts.
- **Outcomes**: Proof of healthy indoor environments; early detection of HVAC failures; ESG reporting support.

### 4. Organized Industrial Zone (OIZ) Environmental Management
- **Target Sectors**: OIZ Management, Free Zones, Industrial Parks, Logistics Hubs.
- **Problem**: Inability to identify which specific tenant in a large industrial zone is responsible for emissions that trigger complaints.
- **Solution**: Perimeter and internal corridor monitoring network. Wind-rose source attribution to identify violators, providing individual tenant portals and shared dashboards.
- **Outcomes**: Evidence-based tenant management; positions the OIZ as a "Green OIZ" attractive to international investment and financing.

### 5. Research, Scientific Studies & Emergency Response
- **Target Sectors**: Universities, Research Institutes, Environmental Agencies, NGOs, Disaster Response Teams.
- **Problem**: Need for flexible, fast-deploying, reliable monitoring equipment for specific campaigns where reference-grade monitors are too expensive or fragile.
- **Solution**: Unit L deploys in under 5 minutes with solar power and cellular transmission. Provides publishable, traceable data (ISO 17025 references).
- **Outcomes**: Publishable data for research; rapid deployment in emergencies (e.g., Kahramanmaraş Earthquake); entry point to larger institutional relationships.

## Commercial Notes
- **Pricing**: USD-denominated for international clients; TRL for Turkish clients. Baseline operational service is ~$700/unit/year. Rental models common for industrial clients.
- **Positioning**: Lead with the customer's pain (complaints, compliance), not features. Airqoon is an ambient/immissions monitoring solution, not a stack emissions (CEMS) solution.
