# Source: Bettair MK2 FAQs (Rev 9, Feb-25)

**Raw file:** `raw/articles/FAQs-Bettair-MK2_rev9_EN.pdf`

## Summary

Frequently Asked Questions document for Bettair's MK2 product. Provides valuable insight into their product strategy, pricing models, and maintenance workflows.

## Key Facts Extracted
- **Sensors:** Measures NO2, NO, O3, CO, SO2, H2S, NH3, VOC, CO2, CH4, HCl. Always includes PM10, PM2.5, PM1, noise, pressure, temperature, humidity.
- **Hardware Strategy:** Uses an easily interchangeable sensor cartridge. Each cartridge is factory calibrated, avoiding the need for complex on-site maintenance.
- **Pricing Model:** 
  - "Zero Maintenance Cost": Factory calibration is baked into the purchase price of nodes/cartridges.
  - "Free Software": Data management and visualization software subscription is included free of charge with the node. Purchasing a replacement cartridge automatically renews the software subscription.
  - EU Connectivity: SIM card and data connection are free for EU and associated countries (pre-configured).
- **Technology:** Uses machine-learning technology for specific data processing, maintaining a stable correlation throughout the sensor's lifespan. Can connect via 3G/4G, Ethernet, or LoRaWAN. Offers public data websites for clients (e.g., public.bettair.city).
