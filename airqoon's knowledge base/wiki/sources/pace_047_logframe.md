# Clean Air Hatay: Post-Earthquake Air Quality Awareness and Protection Project in Schools

**Project Title**: Clean Air Hatay: Post-Earthquake Air Quality Awareness and Protection Project in Schools (PACE047)
**Document Type**: Annex 3: Logical Framework (Logframe)

## Overview
This document outlines the intervention logic, baseline, targets, and activities for the "Clean Air Hatay" project, designed to improve the delivery of public services and enable civic engagement in post-earthquake recovery in the Defne district (Hatay).

## Objectives
- **Overall Impact**: Improve public service delivery by local authorities and enable civic engagement in recovery efforts.
- **Specific Outcome**: Improve the capacity of students, teachers, and municipal staff in Defne to monitor, understand, and respond to air quality risks, leading to protective behaviors and institutional capacity improvements by April 2027.

## Key Outputs & Airqoon's Role
1. **Outdoor Monitoring Network (Output 1)**
   - **Action**: Installation of 10 outdoor PM2.5/PM10 sensors at pilot schools.
   - **Airqoon Role**: Provide contracted services for sensor procurement, installation, and data platform configuration. Develop and publish an interactive district pollution map by September 2026.
2. **Indoor LED Warning Systems (Output 2)**
   - **Action**: Installation of 20 indoor offline LED warning systems across 10 pilot schools.
   - **Airqoon Role**: Provide contracted services for the indoor units and support teacher orientation on system use.
3. **Corsi-Rosenthal Box Air Purifiers (Output 3)**
   - Students will build 20 CR Box devices and deploy them in 20 classrooms. 
   - Devices will run continuously for 2 months.
4. **Metagenomic Analysis (Output 4)**
   - 20 filter samples collected from CR Boxes and submitted for laboratory analysis (heavy metals + microbiological profile) to assess post-earthquake construction dust.
   - Results will inform a scientific report and policy summary.
5. **Capacity Building (Outputs 5 & 6)**
   - 10 mentor teachers and 20 municipal staff (Environment and Health units) will complete intensive training programs.
   - 300 students will participate in awareness workshops, assessed via pre-post surveys.
6. **Replication Guide (Output 7)**
   - Production of a 'Defne Model' replication guide to be shared with at least 5 neighboring municipalities.

## Targets & Timelines
- **Baseline Year**: 2025 (assumed 0 capacity/infrastructure in all indicators).
- **Target Completion Date**: April 2027.
- **Key Milestones**: 
  - Sensor installation by July 2026.
  - Indoor LED systems by August 2026.
  - Metagenomic analysis report by February 2027.
