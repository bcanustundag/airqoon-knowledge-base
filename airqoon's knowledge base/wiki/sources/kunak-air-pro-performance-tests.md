# Kunak AIR Pro Performance Test Results

## Source Metadata
- **File**: `articles/b7a6f0fc2fd37207330aa809fa5557c0304f80e39f6d3b5d09d3dbaaf6d725a2.pdf`
- **Author/Org**: Kunak Technologies
- **Date**: Studies conducted between 2019 and 2023.

## Summary
A compilation of co-location field studies showcasing the performance of the Kunak AIR Pro against reference equipment across various cities (Lille, Stockholm, Barcelona, Mexico City, Le Havre, Madrid, Manchester).

## Test Procedure & Conditions
- Installed 3-4 meters high, next to a reference AQS.
- 2 days stabilization time; no calibration applied during tests.
- Real-time data, no post-processing or recalibration.
- Performance metrics evaluated: R², Accuracy (MAE), 90% Confidence Interval, Expanded Uncertainty (U(exp)), and Availability.

## Relevance to Airqoon
- **Benchmarking**: Kunak is achieving <25% uncertainty for key gases and <50% for PM, meeting Class 1 indicative measurement standards under EU Directives. Airqoon's [[wiki/entities/unit-l|Unit L]] needs to match or exceed these R² and uncertainty metrics to remain competitive.
- **Transparency in Marketing**: Publishing raw field co-location data builds massive credibility. Airqoon should structure its own co-location reports similarly (detailing test procedure, stabilization time, lack of post-processing, and clear DQO metrics).
